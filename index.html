About iSmartLife

iSmartLife is an interdisciplinary platform dedicated to advancing research and public understanding at the intersection of Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS), Yoga, contemplative sciences, neuroscience, physiology, and emerging technologies. The initiative emphasizes academic integrity, experimental rigor, ethical responsibility, and translational impact.

The platform is guided by a vision of integrating classical wisdom traditions with contemporary scientific methodologies to address individual well-being, societal health, and conscious technological development.
